FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from ReviewPro or Revinate?

Three differences. One, we deliver to your inbox and WhatsApp — there's no dashboard you have to log into. Two, our entry price is €79/month instead of €500-2000/month, which makes us viable for independent restaurants and small hotel groups. Three, we cover restaurants and HORECA businesses, not just hotels. Same AI quality, smaller business model.

What data sources do you cover?

Hotels: Google Business Profile, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Expedia, Trip.com, ZenHotels (6 portals). Restaurants: Google Maps and TripAdvisor. Business / HORECA: Google Business Profile. We add new sources by customer request when we have 3+ customers asking for the same one.

What languages do you support?

Reviews are translated and analysed in 18 languages including English, German, French, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Greek, Russian, Dutch, Turkish, Polish, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Arabic, Mandarin, and Japanese. AI-drafted replies output in the language the reviewer used (or your operating language — your choice).

How fast is the 1★ alert?

Within 30 minutes during operating hours. We poll Google and TripAdvisor every 30 minutes for new low-star reviews at your venues. If one appears, you get a WhatsApp + email with the review text, sentiment context, and a pre-drafted diplomatic reply. Outside operating hours, alerts queue for the next morning so we don't wake you at 3am for a fixable review.

Who writes the AI-drafted replies?

Claude AI (Anthropic), with a system prompt we've tuned for hospitality. Every draft is diplomatic-by-default: acknowledges feelings without conceding contested facts, never blames internal staff in public, finds one genuine positive in negative reviews, and ends with a soft return invitation. Drafts are never auto-published. Your team reviews and edits before posting to Google or TripAdvisor. Sensitive content (food safety, legal threats, discrimination claims) is never auto-drafted — it's flagged for manual handling by your team.

What about GDPR?

Reviews and reviewer names are already public on Google and TripAdvisor — we don't republish anything that wasn't already public. We process this data on EU-based servers (Neon Postgres in Frankfurt) and AI processing via Anthropic's European endpoints where available. We sign DPAs with EU customers and never sell or share your data. Right-to-erasure requests are honoured.

Can I switch from my current tool without losing data?

Yes. We backfill 90 days of your historical reviews on day 1 so your trend charts and benchmarks have signal from the moment you start. If you have an export from your current tool, we can also import that. Switching takes a single onboarding call.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep the last 90 days of reports we generated. We stop billing on the day you cancel. We delete your venue config and competitor list. We can export your raw review history on request before deletion. No exit fee, no contract.

Can I see your platform before I commit?

Yes. We have two sample reports above for a hotel and a restaurant — they're real reports with realistic data. For your specific business, we can generate a personalised sample within 60 seconds on a demo call.
