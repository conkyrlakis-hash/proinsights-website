HOW IT WORKS

Read review, draft reply, approve, publish.

For every new review that lands on Google or TripAdvisor, ProInsights produces a draft reply that takes the review's content, the venue's tone of voice, and your house policy into account. The draft appears in the morning report and in the dashboard. You can publish it as-is, edit it, or rewrite it entirely.

ProInsights never auto-publishes. The draft is a starting point — not a finished answer. The job of the AI is to do the first 80% of the writing in your voice, so the human review takes thirty seconds instead of fifteen minutes per reply. The remaining 20% — the personal touch, the gesture of recovery, the in-joke with a regular guest — stays with the person who knows the venue.

BRAND VOICE

A house voice you set, not a generic template.

At onboarding, we ask you ten questions about how you want to sound: warm or formal, regional or international, signed by an owner or by a team, ending with an invitation or with a thank-you. The answers become a tone profile that shapes every draft.

Tone

Warm, formal, witty, understated. Pick one or layer two. The same restaurant can use a slightly different tone for praise versus criticism, so good reviews do not all sound like the same form letter.

Signature

Sign every reply with the owner's name, the manager's name, or a team signature. Match what you already use in person and online. You can also configure different signatures for different shifts or different signed-in users.

Phrases to use and avoid

House phrases you always want ("thank you for choosing us") and corporate clichés you never want ("we appreciate your feedback"). Both are honoured. The ban-list is the feature most operators tell us they care most about.

SMART SNIPPETS

Reusable replies for the recurring situations.

Every restaurant has the same five recurring themes: too noisy, too slow on a Saturday, the wine list, the bread, the dessert. ProInsights learns the snippets you use most and offers them when the same theme comes up. You save time on the routine answers, you spend that time on the replies that need real care.

Snippets are not templates. They are short, configurable building blocks that the AI assembles into a draft that still reads like a person wrote it. You can edit any snippet from the dashboard, and the change propagates to every future draft that uses it.

MULTILINGUAL

Reply in the language the review was written in.

A guest leaving a review in French gets a reply in French. A review in Greek gets a reply in Greek. ProInsights detects the review's language and drafts the reply in the same one, keeping your house voice intact across languages.

Currently supported as standard: English, Greek, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Polish, Dutch and Romanian. Other languages on request. For Cypriot restaurants specifically, the Greek and Russian quality has been tuned with native speakers — reply drafts read naturally rather than as machine translations.

SENSITIVE CONTENT

Some replies are not for AI to draft.

Reviews mentioning food safety, allergy, hygiene, illness, injury, or legal threats are deliberately not auto-drafted. ProInsights flags them, routes them to a separate human-handling queue, and leaves the reply for a senior person on your team. We do not hand the keys to AI for content that can hurt your business in court.

You can add your own custom triggers — a specific dish, a named staff member, a complaint type — that should also route to human-only handling. The triggers are managed from the dashboard and you can change them at any time.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Does the AI ever publish a reply on its own?
No. Every reply is drafted, queued, and waits for your approval. ProInsights never publishes to Google or TripAdvisor without an explicit sign-off from your team.
What languages do you support?
English, Greek, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Russian, Polish, Dutch and Romanian as standard. The draft is generated in whatever language the review was written in. Other languages are available on request.
Can I keep the AI from replying to certain types of reviews?
Yes. Reviews touching food safety, allergy, illness, injury, hygiene or legal threats are not auto-drafted by default. You can also add custom triggers — for example, any mention of a specific dish or staff member — that should route to human-only handling.
Can different team members have different tones?
Yes. You can configure one voice for owner-signed replies and another for manager-signed replies. Both are saved as separate tone profiles.
Is this included in all tiers?
AI-drafted replies are a Pro tier feature for restaurants. On Basic and Advanced you still receive the daily review summary and themes, but not AI reply drafts.
