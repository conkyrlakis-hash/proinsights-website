GUEST REVIEW MONITORING

Seven sources, one report.

ProInsights pulls new guest reviews every night from the channels that matter to European hotels and consolidates them into one morning briefing. No tab switching, no dashboard fatigue, no Monday-morning scramble.

  • Google — Business Profile reviews and rating
  • TripAdvisor — guest reviews, ranking, traveller photos
  • Booking.com — guest reviews
  • Expedia — guest reviews
  • Trip.com — guest reviews
  • ZenHotels — guest reviews
  • HolidayCheck — guest reviews

Each review is scored for sentiment, tagged with themes, and routed to the right department in the report. Nothing else is claimed. If a source is not on this list, we do not pretend to read it.

SENTIMENT BY DEPARTMENT

Read the hotel the way you run it.

Reviews are sliced by the categories your team owns, so the right line manager sees the right signal.

Front of house

Check-in, check-out, concierge, F&B service, named staff. The signal a front-office manager actually needs.

Housekeeping

Cleanliness, room condition, linen, bathroom, smell. Trend lines per week and per month so you can spot drift early.

Breakfast and F&B

Breakfast variety, quality, opening times. Restaurant and bar mentioned separately. Praised and criticised dishes called out by name.

Room product

Comfort, size, bed, soundproofing, AC, view. The structural complaints that capex needs to see, not the ops team.

Location

Walkability, transport, distance to beach, distance to centre. Useful when you are positioning against properties with a different geography.

Value and price

Whether guests felt the rate matched the experience. Cross-referenced with your verified rate position against competitors.

COMPETITOR BENCHMARKING

Five to ten named competitors, watched every day.

You pick your competitors at sign-up. We track their reviews across the same seven sources, daily, and surface the side-by-side: average rating, review volume, themes trending up at their property, themes trending down at yours.

Expert tier adds a weekly Strengths and Weaknesses analysis — what each competitor is being praised for, what each is being criticised for, in plain English. The quarterly deep-dive briefing maps your competitive position over time so you can see drift before it shows in your rate.

VERIFIED RATE COMPARISON

Rates with provenance — or no rate at all.

Pro and Expert tiers include rate comparison against your competitor set. Every rate we display is verified with source, timestamp, occupancy, currency, and a confidence status. Rates are hidden when data quality is not safe to display. We do not show estimates dressed up as facts.

Expert tier extends this into a room-rate market analysis: room-category match, demand-day pricing patterns, and parity-violation flags across up to six competitors. The aim is to give your revenue manager a defensible position to work from — not a number nobody can stand behind.

DAILY MANAGER REPORT

One email. Three lines at the top. Everything else below.

The morning report opens with a three-line AI brief — what changed overnight, what needs action today, what to watch this week. Then the detail: new reviews by source, department sentiment shifts, theme movement, competitor moves, and any one-star review that demands an urgent alert response.

On Pro, the same brief lands on your phone as a WhatsApp message. One phone is included on Pro; additional phones are €10 a month each. Unlimited email recipients on Pro and above so your DOM, front office manager and ownership all see the same thing at the same time.

DASHBOARD

A web dashboard you can ignore most of the time.

Every report you have ever received is archived, searchable, and exportable as PDF. Filter by department, by source, by date range. Pull a board pack in two clicks. But the dashboard is not the workflow — the email and the WhatsApp are the workflow. The dashboard is for the deeper look on Friday afternoon.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Which review sources do you monitor for hotels?
Seven: Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Expedia, Trip.com, ZenHotels and HolidayCheck. That is the full list. If a source matters to you and is not listed, ask us — we will be honest about whether it is coming or not.
How is your rate comparison different from a rate-shopper tool?
We are not a rate shopper. We display rates we have verified with source, timestamp, occupancy, currency and a confidence status, and we hide them when data quality is not safe to display. The aim is intelligence you can defend in a revenue meeting, not a daily number that may or may not be real.
How does ProInsights compare to Shiji ReviewPro?
ReviewPro is a powerful enterprise platform built for global chains with central reputation teams. ProInsights is built for independent hotels and boutique groups that want a daily briefing on email and WhatsApp without an enterprise rollout. See the side-by-side on our ReviewPro alternative page.
Can our DOM, front office manager and ownership all receive the report?
Yes. Advanced tier supports up to 5 recipients. Pro and Expert support unlimited recipients. One WhatsApp phone is included on Pro and above; extra phones are €10 a month each.
What is the 1-star alert?
On Pro and above, any new one-star review across your monitored sources triggers a WhatsApp and email alert within 90 minutes during operating hours. Reviews mentioning food safety, allergy, hygiene or legal threats are auto-escalated and never auto-replied.
