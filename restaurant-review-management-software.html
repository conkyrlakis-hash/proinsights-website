DAILY MONITORING

Every new Google and TripAdvisor review, before service.

ProInsights checks your Google Business Profile and TripAdvisor listing every night and assembles a single morning report. New reviews are read by AI, scored for sentiment, tagged with themes, and ranked by what actually needs your attention. The format is an email and an optional WhatsApp message — not a dashboard you have to remember to open.

  • New reviews across Google Maps and TripAdvisor, daily
  • Star rating delta versus yesterday, last week, last month
  • Themes auto-detected (service, food, price, ambience, noise, wait time)
  • Sentiment per review, in plain English

DISH, STAFF, SERVICE MENTIONS

Find out which dishes guests are talking about — and which staff get named.

Restaurants are not hotels. The signal hides in named dishes and named team members. ProInsights surfaces both, automatically.

Dish leaderboard

The dishes mentioned most in the last 7, 30 and 90 days. Praised dishes ranked separately from criticised ones. Useful when you are deciding what to push, what to retire, and what the kitchen needs to fix.

Staff mentions

Named team members are extracted and counted. You see who is being thanked publicly and who is being singled out in complaints. Hand them the printout at the next briefing.

Service categories

Welcome, pace, attentiveness, knowledge, billing, and farewell — each tracked as its own theme so you can see where the friction is, not just that it exists.

1-STAR ALERT

A one-star review should not wait until the morning email.

On Pro and above, any new one-star review triggers an instant WhatsApp and email alert within 90 minutes of appearing, during your operating hours. The alert includes the full review text, the platform, the timestamp, and a flag if the review mentions food safety, allergy, hygiene or a legal threat — sensitive cases are escalated and never auto-replied.

This is the difference between hearing about a bad review from a guest on social media versus catching it before lunch service. It is the single most-used feature for restaurants that take reputation seriously.

COMPETITOR TRACKING

Five to ten named competitors, watched daily.

Name your direct competitors at sign-up. ProInsights tracks their new reviews and ratings every day, so you see what they are getting praised for, what they are getting criticised for, and how your rating compares week over week.

Advanced tier tracks five competitors. Pro tier tracks ten. You get a weekly side-by-side that fits on one screen — average rating, review volume, themes trending up, themes trending down. No data extracts to download, no formulas to maintain.

AI REPLY DRAFTS

Diplomatic replies, drafted in your house voice.

On Pro, every new review can come back with a draft reply written in the tone you set during onboarding — warm, formal, regional, multilingual. You approve, edit, or rewrite. ProInsights never publishes without you.

Replies that touch food safety, allergy, hygiene or any legal threat are deliberately not auto-drafted. Those go to a human escalation queue. We do not hand the keys to AI for content that can hurt your business.

DASHBOARD AND ARCHIVE

A web dashboard you do not have to live in.

The dashboard is there when you want it — every daily, weekly and monthly report archived, searchable, and exportable. Filter by date range. Pull staff mention counts for a performance review. Send a board pack as a PDF. But the dashboard is the backup, not the workflow. The email and WhatsApp are the workflow.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Which platforms do you monitor for restaurants?
Google Maps and TripAdvisor. Those are the two platforms that actually move bookings for independent restaurants in Europe. We do not pretend to monitor sources we cannot reliably read.
How quickly will I get my first report?
Within 24 hours of signing the order form. Onboarding is a 15-minute call where you hand us your Google Business Profile URL and TripAdvisor URL, name your competitors, and pick the tone of voice for AI replies.
Will the AI ever reply to a review without me seeing it first?
No. ProInsights drafts replies. You approve or edit them. We never publish on your behalf. Reviews touching food safety, allergy or legal threats are explicitly not auto-drafted at all — they are flagged for human review.
Can I track dishes by exact name?
Yes. Named dishes are extracted automatically from review text and grouped by mention count, sentiment, and trend. You can also add your menu items at onboarding so we match informal mentions to the canonical name.
