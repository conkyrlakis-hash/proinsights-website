TripAdvisor review monitoring for hotels — built around how the ranking actually moves

TripAdvisor still drives bookings, especially for independent hotels and boutique groups in Mediterranean Europe. ProInsights reads every new TripAdvisor review for your property overnight, watches your ranking, surfaces sentiment by department, and benchmarks you against the competitor set you choose.

DAILY TRIPADVISOR MONITORING

Every new review, every morning, no logging in.

Each night ProInsights checks your TripAdvisor listing for new reviews and rating changes. By 8am you have an email summary covering what was written, the sentiment of each new review, the themes they touched, and any movement on your overall rating.

The report is built so the GM can read it in two minutes and the DOM can read it for ten. Nothing is buried, nothing is padded. Reviews are listed with the most consequential first — a one-bubble review with a food-safety complaint comes above a five-bubble review praising the breakfast spread, because that is the order in which you need to act on them.

The format is the same whether you receive two new reviews a week or twenty a day. We have built it around the principle that you should never have to scan through padding to find the thing that matters.

SENTIMENT BY DEPARTMENT

Service. Cleanliness. Value. Location. Room. Sleep quality.

TripAdvisor reviews are categorised against the dimensions hotel operators actually run their teams against. Each new review is routed to the right department in the report, with a sentiment score and named-staff mentions where they appear.

You can see at a glance whether the housekeeping trend is improving, whether breakfast complaints have spiked, and whether a named team member is collecting public thanks — the kind of intelligence that should drive your weekly ops meeting. The DOM gets the commercial picture, the front-office manager gets the service picture, and the head of housekeeping gets a clean view of every cleanliness mention in the last seven days without having to read every full review.

RANKING WATCH

Know when your ranking moves, not after.

TripAdvisor ranking shifts quietly. ProInsights tracks your position in your destination's hotel list and flags meaningful changes — both up and down. You see the movement in context: the ranking, the rating, and the review volume that drove it.

A small ranking gain over a quiet week is not the same story as a large drop following a critical-review cluster. We do not just report the number — we set it next to the cause, so the morning brief gives you a defensible explanation for ownership.

COMPETITOR BENCHMARKING

Five to ten competitors, watched the same way.

You name your direct competitors at sign-up. ProInsights tracks their TripAdvisor reviews and ratings every day on the same cadence as yours. The weekly side-by-side covers average rating, review volume, themes trending up at their property, themes trending down at yours.

Expert tier extends this with a strengths and weaknesses analysis per competitor, written in plain English. The aim is intelligence your commercial team can act on, not a chart that needs interpreting. If a peer property has had a sudden cluster of cleanliness complaints, your DOM should be able to read that fact in one sentence and decide what to do with it.

1-STAR ALERT

A bad review needs you within thirty minutes, not tomorrow.

On Pro and above, any new one-star TripAdvisor review (or one-bubble in TripAdvisor's terms) triggers a WhatsApp and email alert within 30 minutes during operating hours. Reviews mentioning sensitive topics — illness, injury, allergy, legal threat — are flagged for human handling and never auto-replied. You get the alert with full context and a starting position for a reply, but the publication is always under your control.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Do you watch TripAdvisor ranking, not just reviews?
Yes. We track your position in your destination's hotel ranking and flag meaningful movements. The morning report shows ranking alongside rating and review volume so changes have context.
Can I track restaurant-style TripAdvisor listings as well?
Yes — TripAdvisor is a standard source for both our hotel and restaurant verticals. Restaurants on TripAdvisor are monitored exactly the same way as hotels.
How is sentiment scored?
Every review is read by Claude AI and scored on sentiment per theme, not just overall. A review that praises the room but criticises breakfast will register both signals correctly.
What about TripAdvisor sub-scores like service and location?
We mirror TripAdvisor's category structure in the report and track each as a separate sub-score so you can see trends over time at category level.
Do you draft replies to TripAdvisor reviews?
On Pro and above, yes — diplomatic drafts in your house voice, in the language the review was written in. You approve before anything goes live. Reviews touching sensitive topics are routed to humans only.
