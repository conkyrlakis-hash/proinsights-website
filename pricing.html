WHICH TIER FITS

A short guide to picking the right tier.

Basic (€79) is the right starting point for a small independent hotel that wants the daily report and theme detection on the six standard portals, with no competitor tracking yet. Most operators move off Basic within a few months as the value of competitor benchmarking becomes obvious.

Advanced (€139) adds five competitors tracked daily, plus the weekly and monthly reports for the wider management team. The €60 step up from Basic is the cheapest competitor-benchmark you will find in the European market and where most boutique hotels settle.

Pro (€189) is where most serious operators end up. WhatsApp morning summary, AI-drafted replies in your house voice, verified rate comparison, ten competitors, and the 90-minute one-star alert. Pro is the tier the platform is designed around.

Expert (€249) is for properties that need full daily competitor monitoring, room-rate market analysis with up to six competitors, and the weekly Strengths and Weaknesses analysis. Typically five-star and boutique properties competing in a tight, named peer set.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Why is restaurant pricing not published?
Restaurant venues vary enormously in review volume and competitor scope. We would rather quote you a number we can stand behind than publish a list price that misrepresents what we will charge in practice. Email us and we will respond within one working day.
Is there a setup fee?
No. There is no setup fee on any tier or any vertical.
How does the 30-day money-back work?
If at 30 days you decide ProInsights is not earning its keep, we refund the subscription you have paid. No exit fee, no question, no friction. You keep the reports you have received.
How does the multi-property discount work?
Your first property is at list price. Each additional property is at 20% off the same tier — so Pro at €189 becomes €151 from your second property onwards.
Can I mix tiers across properties?
Yes. A group might run the flagship hotel on Expert and three sister properties on Pro. The 20% multi-property discount applies to each property at its own tier.
Are there annual discounts?
Not currently — we prefer monthly billing because it keeps both sides honest. If you want to discuss an annual arrangement for a multi-property group, get in touch.
Do you charge per user?
No. We charge per property, per month. Email recipients on Advanced are capped at five; on Pro and above they are unlimited. WhatsApp phones beyond the first on Pro and above are €10 per phone per month.

Want to see what guests and competitors are saying tomorrow morning?

Pick a 15-minute slot. We hand you a working report from your venues by tomorrow morning.
