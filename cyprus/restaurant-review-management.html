BUILT IN CYPRUS

A platform made for the way Cyprus actually does hospitality.

Cypriot restaurants run on a seasonal rhythm — the summer months in Ayia Napa, Protaras and Paphos are a different operation from the winter trade in Nicosia and Limassol. Reviews swing accordingly. ProInsights is built in Cyprus, by a team who has worked in the local F&B industry, and it understands that rhythm.

The morning report works in English, Greek and Russian — the three languages your guests are most likely to leave reviews in. AI replies are drafted in the language of the review, so a Greek-speaking guest leaving a Greek-language review on Google gets a reply in Greek.

REGIONS WE COVER

Every region of Cyprus, restaurants of every size.

Limassol

From the Marina fine-dining rooms to the old-town tavernas, Limassol restaurants face the most international guest mix on the island — and the highest competitor density. ProInsights tracks ten named competitors daily on Pro.

Nicosia

A largely local market with year-round trade, Nicosia restaurants live or die by repeat custom and local Google reviews. The daily brief catches changes fast so you can address them at the next service.

Larnaca

Mixed local-and-tourist demand around the seafront and the salt lake. ProInsights handles the seasonal switch between TripAdvisor-heavy summer months and Google-heavy winter months without you having to retune anything.

Paphos

High-volume tourist trade with a long season. The 1-star alert is especially valuable here — catching a bad review at lunch versus the following morning often makes the difference for a venue running fifty covers a sitting.

Ayia Napa & Protaras

Seasonal volume that scales hard between May and October. ProInsights handles the volume swing without you having to upgrade or downgrade tier through the year — you pay for the season you are operating in.

Troodos and the villages

Lower review volume but higher stakes per review for small mountain tavernas. The dish leaderboard and named-staff mentions are typically more useful at this scale than the competitor tracking.
