REGIONS WE COVER

From Limassol seafront to Paphos resorts.

Limassol

City-and-resort hybrid market with the highest international guest density on the island. Up to ten named competitors tracked daily on Pro, including the obvious five-star peer set.

Paphos

Long-season resort trade with deep Booking.com and Expedia volumes. The daily report catches the weekly rhythm of arrivals and departures so trends do not get lost in noise.

Larnaca

Airport-adjacent and seafront properties with mixed business-and-leisure demand. The verified rate comparison is especially useful for properties competing on weekend price.

Ayia Napa & Protaras

Highly seasonal beach properties. Pro and Expert tiers help you watch the competitor set tighten through July and August and the rate environment shift in real time.

Nicosia

The business-travel core of Cyprus. Lower review volume per property but high stakes per review. Sentiment by department surfaces F&B, meeting facilities and front-office issues quickly.

Troodos and rural Cyprus

Smaller properties with thinner review streams but distinct guest profiles. The dashboard archive matters more here for trend analysis over a long season.

VERIFIED RATE COMPARISON

Rates with provenance — or hidden.

Pro and Expert tiers include rate comparison against your named competitor set. Every rate displayed comes with source, timestamp, occupancy, currency, and a confidence status. Rates are hidden when data quality is not safe to display. We do not bluff numbers your revenue manager has to defend in a meeting.

Expert tier extends this into a full room-rate market analysis — up to six competitors, room-category match, demand-day pricing patterns, parity violations flagged automatically. The aim is intelligence your DOM can use, not a data dump that needs interpretation.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Which sources matter most for Cyprus hotels?
Booking.com is the largest single source for most Cypriot hotels, with TripAdvisor and Google second and third depending on the property. Expedia matters for North American and UK demand, Trip.com for Asia, HolidayCheck and ZenHotels for the German and Eastern-European tour-operator markets. We cover all seven as first-class sources.
Can I see verified competitor rates without signing up?
We will show you sample rate comparisons during the demo, drawn from your actual competitor set on the day. Each rate carries source, timestamp, occupancy, currency and confidence — so you can see the verification trail before you commit.
Can the morning report go to ownership in Athens or London?
Yes. On Pro and Expert there is no recipient cap, so the GM, DOM, ownership and asset manager can all be on the same brief. The WhatsApp summary on Pro covers one phone — additional phones are €10 a month each.
