Google review monitoring for restaurants — every morning, in your inbox

Google reviews move covers more than any other platform for independent restaurants. ProInsights reads every new Google review for your restaurant overnight, scores it, tags it, ranks it, and delivers a single short email at 8am — plus an optional WhatsApp message and a 30-minute alert on any one-star review.

WHY GOOGLE FIRST

Google is where the booking starts.

For most independent restaurants in Europe, a guest decides whether to book within ten seconds of seeing your Google Business Profile. The star rating, the most recent reviews, and how you respond to them shape that decision. If you do not have a system that reads every new Google review on the day it lands, you are managing your reputation in arrears.

ProInsights is built around that one fact. Google Maps is a first-class source — not bolted on as an afterthought, not lumped into a generic crawler. We read Google reviews as they appear, sort them by sentiment, and tell you in three lines what changed overnight.

The platform is built specifically for restaurants that do not have a marketing department in the back office. The morning email is short enough that a busy owner-operator can read it before service prep, but detailed enough that the head chef and the floor manager can act on it together. We have spent a lot of time deciding what to leave out, not just what to put in.

DAILY BRIEF

What you get every morning at 8am.

The three-line summary

What changed overnight, what to act on today, what to watch this week. Written by Claude AI in plain English. No charts to interpret, no jargon, no marketing language.

Every new Google review

Full text, star rating, sentiment, themes, named dishes, named staff. Sorted so the urgent ones are at the top of the email, not buried at the bottom.

Rating delta

Your current Google star rating versus yesterday, last week, last month. The numbers your reservations team should know without being asked.

1-STAR ALERT

When a one-star review lands, you hear about it fast.

On Pro tier and above, any new one-star Google review triggers a WhatsApp and email alert within 30 minutes during operating hours. The alert includes the full review text and a flag if the review mentions food safety, allergy, hygiene or a legal threat. Those reviews are routed to a human escalation queue — never auto-replied.

This is the feature most operators say they wish they had during the last bad week. Catching a one-star at 12:15 versus 16:45 is the difference between a recovered guest and a public argument. A reply written calmly within an hour reads completely differently from one written defensively two days later.

AI REPLY DRAFTS

A draft reply, in your voice, ready when you are.

On Pro, every new Google review can come with a draft reply written in the tone you set at onboarding. Warm or formal. English, Greek, French, German, Italian, Spanish, or any language the original review was written in. You approve, edit, or rewrite. ProInsights does not publish without your sign-off.

The replies are not generic. The tone profile we build at onboarding makes a measurable difference — a Greek-village taverna does not sound like a Michelin-listed restaurant, and the drafts reflect that. You can also keep house phrases and ban corporate clichés, so the voice stays yours across hundreds of replies.

DISHES AND STAFF

Find the dishes and team members being talked about.

Google reviews are full of specifics: a server's name, a dish, a wait time, a corkage charge. ProInsights extracts those automatically and ranks them. You see which dishes are getting praised, which are getting criticised, and which team members are being singled out by name — positively or negatively. Useful for the next pre-shift briefing.

Praised dishes are listed separately from criticised dishes, so the same dish can appear in both lists if guests are split on it. The named-staff section is one many managers print and bring to weekly briefings — it makes it easy to thank a server publicly and to have an honest conversation with one who keeps getting flagged for pace.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Do I need a Google API key or special access?
No. We use your public Google Business Profile URL. You hand us the link, we do the rest.
How quickly does a new Google review show up in my report?
New reviews are pulled overnight and appear in the next morning's report. One-star reviews on Pro and above also trigger an instant alert within 30 minutes during operating hours.
Can the AI reply to Google reviews on my behalf?
ProInsights drafts replies. You approve or edit them before they go live. We never publish without your sign-off, and we never draft replies to reviews mentioning food safety, allergy or legal threats — those are human-only.
Will this work for a restaurant with very few reviews?
Yes. The daily report is just as useful for a restaurant getting two reviews a week as one getting twenty. With low volume, the trend lines become more important than the daily count.
Does this also monitor TripAdvisor?
Yes — restaurants on ProInsights are monitored on both Google Maps and TripAdvisor as standard.
