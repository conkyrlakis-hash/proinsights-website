Hotel competitor benchmarking software — five to ten competitors, watched daily

Pick the hotels you actually compete with. ProInsights watches their reviews, ratings, sentiment, and verified rates every day, and shows you a side-by-side that fits on one screen. No CSV exports, no formulas, no quarterly dashboards that arrive too late.

NAMED COMPETITORS

You pick the set. We track it every day.

At sign-up you name five competitors (Advanced) or ten (Pro and Expert). These are real hotels, not algorithmic suggestions. We then monitor each one across the seven review sources we cover: Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Expedia, Trip.com, ZenHotels and HolidayCheck.

Every new review at every tracked competitor is read overnight, sentiment-scored, and theme-tagged. The result lands in your weekly competitor section — and on Expert tier, in the daily competitor review monitoring stream.

You can change your competitor set at any time. If a new resort opens down the road in May and starts taking your shoulder-season demand, swap one of your existing competitors out and add the new one in. Changes apply from the next morning's report.

SIDE-BY-SIDE

One screen, six rows, the comparison your commercial team needs.

The competitor section of your weekly report is a single side-by-side table. For your property and each competitor, you see:

  • Current average rating across each source
  • Review volume in the last 7 and 30 days
  • Top three themes trending up
  • Top three themes trending down
  • Most-mentioned staff and most-mentioned dishes / amenities
  • Verified rate position (Pro and Expert, hotel only)

Nothing in there that needs a quant to interpret. Nothing missing that a GM would have to chase. The table is exportable to PDF in two clicks for the weekly commercial meeting, and the dashboard archives every version so you can show ownership the trajectory over months.

STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES

What each competitor is being praised and criticised for, in plain English.

Expert tier adds a weekly Strengths and Weaknesses analysis per competitor. Claude AI reads all of their new reviews and produces a one-paragraph summary of what guests love about that hotel and what they complain about. You can read your five most-relevant competitors in two minutes.

The quarterly deep-dive briefing maps movement over time so you can see where a competitor is closing on you, where they are slipping, and where the market is shifting against the whole set. The briefing is written in narrative form — paragraphs, not bullet points — because the patterns that matter are usually structural, not transactional.

VERIFIED RATE COMPARISON

Rates with provenance, or hidden.

On Pro and Expert, the side-by-side includes verified rate comparison against your competitor set. Every rate displayed comes with source, timestamp, occupancy, currency, and a confidence status. Rates are hidden when data quality is not safe to display. We do not show speculative numbers dressed as facts.

Expert tier extends this into a room-rate market analysis: room-category match, demand-day pricing patterns, and parity violations across up to six competitors. The output is built to be useful in a revenue meeting — a defensible position your DOM can quote without disclaimers.

DAILY COMPETITOR MONITORING

Catch a competitor's bad week the day it starts.

Expert tier turns competitor monitoring into a daily stream. Every new review at every tracked competitor appears in your daily report, with sentiment and themes. If a rival hotel has a sudden cluster of cleanliness complaints, you see it the same morning their housekeeping manager does — sometimes earlier.

This is the level of intelligence that lets you move on a temporary opening in the market before the rival has time to recover. It is also the level that tells you when a peer property is genuinely outperforming you on a dimension where you used to lead — which is the harder conversation to have internally, but the more useful one.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

How many competitors can I track?
Advanced tier tracks five competitors. Pro and Expert track ten. You can change the named set at any time.
Where do you pull competitor reviews from?
The same seven sources we use for your own property: Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Expedia, Trip.com, ZenHotels and HolidayCheck.
Are competitor rates included?
Verified rate comparison is included on Pro and Expert (hotel only). Every rate carries source, timestamp, occupancy, currency and a confidence status, and rates are hidden when data quality is not safe to display.
How often is competitor data refreshed?
Daily on Advanced and Pro for the weekly side-by-side. Daily review-level monitoring is a feature of Expert tier.
Can I pick competitors in different cities?
Yes. The competitor set is whatever you name. Geography is up to you.
What if a competitor's listing is hard to scrape?
If we cannot reliably read a source for a given competitor, we will say so and we will not pretend to have data we do not have. You can always swap that competitor for another.

Want to see what guests and competitors are saying tomorrow morning?

Pick a 15-minute slot. We hand you a working report from your venues by tomorrow morning.
