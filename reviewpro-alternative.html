A ReviewPro alternative for operators, not enterprises

ReviewPro is a powerful platform with a long track record at large hotel groups. It is built around enterprise needs: central reputation teams, complex tagging hierarchies, integrations with CRS and PMS, multi-region rollouts. ProInsights is built differently — for the independent hotel, the boutique group, the restaurant operator who wants a daily brief on WhatsApp and email without the enterprise weight. This page lays out where each one fits.

Book a demo See sample report

WHO EACH IS FOR

Two products, two operating models.

If you run a 200-property chain with a central reputation team in Madrid or Singapore, ReviewPro is probably the right answer. It is built around that operating model — dedicated analysts, deep integrations, a quarterly review cycle led by a reputation function.

If you run a single independent hotel, a small boutique group, or a busy restaurant, you do not have a central reputation team. You have a GM, an F&B director, and an owner who would like to know what changed overnight in the time it takes to drink a coffee. ProInsights is built around that operating model.

WHEN PROINSIGHTS IS THE RIGHT CHOICE

Five honest signals.

  • You are an independent hotel or a boutique group of one to ten properties
  • The GM and owner want to read the brief themselves — they are not going to delegate it to a reputation analyst
  • You want the morning summary on WhatsApp, on your phone, before service
  • You do not need integrations into central CRS, PMS or BI stacks
  • You want monthly billing, no setup fee, and the ability to cancel within thirty days

WHEN REVIEWPRO IS THE RIGHT CHOICE

Three honest signals.

  • You operate dozens of properties under a central reputation team
  • You need deep integration into a PMS, CRS or BI environment as a hard requirement
  • Your buying cycle is built around annual enterprise contracts and a procurement-led RFP

If any of those describe you, ReviewPro is a serious answer and we are happy to say so.

SWITCHING FROM REVIEWPRO

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Is ProInsights cheaper than ReviewPro?
Almost certainly, yes — but the products are not exact substitutes. ProInsights is priced from €79 to €249 per property per month. ReviewPro is enterprise-negotiated and the two solve overlapping but distinct problems for different buyers.
Do you integrate with PMS or CRS?
Not currently. We focus on public review data — Google, TripAdvisor, Booking, Expedia, Trip.com, ZenHotels and HolidayCheck — and on getting the morning brief into your team's hands. If deep PMS or CRS integration is a hard requirement, ReviewPro is a better fit.
What review sources do you cover that ReviewPro also covers?
Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Expedia, Trip.com, ZenHotels and HolidayCheck. ReviewPro covers additional sources we do not claim. We list ours explicitly and only those.
Can a group with 30+ properties use ProInsights?
Yes, technically — multi-property discount applies — but if the buying decision sits with a central reputation function and an enterprise procurement process, you should evaluate ReviewPro as well. We will tell you honestly when we think the other product is a better fit.
Do you have a migration assistant for ReviewPro customers?
Not as a productised offering, but onboarding for switchers is the same as for new customers — 15 minutes on a call, first real report within 24 hours, ReviewPro stays live until you decide to commit.

Pick a 15-minute slot. We hand you a working report from your venues by tomorrow morning.
