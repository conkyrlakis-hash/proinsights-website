TERMS OF SERVICE

The agreement, in plain language.

Last updated: 11 May 2026 · Version 1.0 · Draft pending legal review.

The short version

  • Month-to-month. No annual contracts unless you want one and ask for a discount.
  • Cancel anytime. Effective at the end of your current billing month. No exit fee.
  • 30-day money-back. If we haven't delivered value in 30 days, we refund you.
  • We never auto-publish replies on your behalf. Drafts are drafts. You publish.
  • You own your data. We process it under instruction; we don't sell or repurpose it.

1. The service

ProInsights provides a SaaS review-intelligence service: we collect public reviews for venues you specify, analyse them with AI, and deliver daily/weekly/monthly reports by email (and WhatsApp on Pro/Expert tiers). The current tier list, prices, and feature set are defined on the pricing section of our homepage and in our internal TIERS document, which is the source of truth.

2. Account & onboarding

  • To start, you provide: business name, venues to track, competitor list, contact email(s), WhatsApp phone (if applicable), operating language and timezone.
  • Your first daily report is delivered within 24 hours of signature.
  • You authorise us to fetch public review data for the venues you specified, via SerpAPI and similar third-party services.

3. Fees & billing

  • Pricing is per tenant, per month, in EUR. See /pricing.
  • Multi-property discount: 20% off second and subsequent properties.
  • Restaurant + Business pricing is custom — email us for a quote.
  • Billing today is monthly invoice. When Stripe integration ships (planned 2026), card-on-file becomes available at customer option.
  • Invoices are payable within 14 days. Overdue invoices may pause service after 30 days; we'll email a reminder first.
  • Prices may change for new customers at any time. Existing customers get 60 days notice before any price change applies to them.

4. Cancellation & refunds

  • Cancel anytime by emailing hello@proinsights.com.cy. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing month.
  • 30-day money-back guarantee: if you cancel within 30 days of your first paid invoice because the service didn't deliver value, we refund the full amount. No questions, no exit fee.
  • After 30 days, fees already paid are not refundable, but you keep service to the end of the billing month.
  • On cancellation, your tenant data is deleted within 14 days unless you request a longer retention or export.

5. Service-level expectations

  • Daily report delivery: within 30 minutes of your configured local schedule (default 08:00) on at least 95% of business days, measured monthly. Missed deliveries are credited proportionally.
  • 1★ alert (Pro/Expert): within 30 minutes of detection during your operating hours. Outside operating hours, alerts batch into the next morning report.
  • This is a best-effort commitment, not an enterprise SLA. We're not an ISP. Outages happen — when they do, we don't pretend they didn't.

6. AI-drafted replies — your responsibility

  • AI-drafted replies are suggestions only. Nothing we generate is auto-published to Google, TripAdvisor, or any other platform.
  • You review and edit every draft before publishing. We provide a "Copy Draft" affordance in your daily report; the act of pasting + publishing is yours.
  • Sensitive reviews (food safety claims, legal threats, discrimination claims, etc.) are flagged for manual handling and intentionally not drafted by AI.
  • You remain solely responsible for the public reputation impact of any reply you choose to publish.

7. Acceptable use

You agree not to use ProInsights to:

  • Process data for venues you don't own or don't have explicit authorisation to monitor
  • Scrape or republish review content outside the report email recipients you specified
  • Generate replies impersonating another business
  • Re-sell our service or expose it as a white-label without a separate reseller agreement
  • Use the service for unlawful purposes

8. Intellectual property

  • The ProInsights name, brand assets, and platform code are ours.
  • Your venue config, brand assets, and review data remain yours.
  • Aggregated, anonymised insights (e.g. "average response rate in EU restaurants") may be used in our marketing without identifying you.

9. Data & privacy

See our Privacy Policy and GDPR statement. The privacy policy is incorporated into this agreement by reference.

10. Confidentiality

  • We will not share your venue list, competitor list, or report contents with any third party except sub-processors necessary to deliver the service (see Privacy Policy).
  • You agree not to publicly share the prices we agreed to in any custom-quoted arrangement (Restaurant or Business tiers, multi-property packages).

11. Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by Cypriot law, our aggregate liability for any claim arising from the service is capped at the fees you paid us in the 12 months preceding the claim. We are not liable for indirect or consequential damages (lost profit, lost reputation, lost bookings).

12. Termination by us

We may terminate or suspend service immediately if you:

  • Violate the acceptable use clause
  • Have an invoice 60+ days overdue
  • Use the service in a way that damages our infrastructure or third-party providers

In any other case we'll give you at least 30 days notice.

13. Changes to these terms

Material changes are emailed to all customers with at least 30 days notice. Continued use after the notice period counts as acceptance. The "Last updated" date is the source of truth.

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of Cyprus. Disputes go to the courts of Cyprus, unless EU consumer law gives you a stronger forum.
