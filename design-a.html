QUESTIONS.
ANSWERS.

How is ProInsights different from ReviewPro or Revinate?

Three things. One: we deliver to email and WhatsApp — no dashboard to log into. Two: €79/month entry instead of €500–2000/month, so it's viable for independent restaurants and small hotel groups. Three: we cover restaurants and HORECA, not just hotels. Same AI quality, smaller business model.

What data sources do you cover?

Hotels: Google Business Profile, TripAdvisor, Booking.com, Expedia, Trip.com, ZenHotels. Restaurants: Google Maps and TripAdvisor. Business / HORECA: Google Business Profile.

What languages do you support?

Reviews are translated and analysed in 18 languages including English, German, French, Italian, Spanish, Greek, Russian, Dutch, Turkish, Polish, Arabic, Mandarin and Japanese. AI-drafted replies output in the language the reviewer used, or your operating language — your choice.

How fast is the 1-star alert?

Within 30 minutes during operating hours. We poll Google and TripAdvisor every 30 minutes for new low-star reviews. You get a WhatsApp + email with the review text and a pre-drafted reply. Outside operating hours, alerts batch into the next morning so we don't wake you at 3am.

Who writes the AI-drafted replies?

Claude AI (Anthropic), with a system prompt tuned for hospitality. Every draft is diplomatic-by-default: acknowledges feelings without conceding contested facts, never blames internal staff in public, finds one genuine positive, ends with a soft return invitation. Drafts are never auto-published. Sensitive content (food safety, legal threats, discrimination) is never auto-drafted.

What about GDPR?

Reviews and reviewer names are already public on Google and TripAdvisor. We process on EU servers (Neon Postgres, Frankfurt). DPAs available on request. See our GDPR statement.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Email us and cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing month. No exit fee. First 30 days come with a money-back guarantee.
