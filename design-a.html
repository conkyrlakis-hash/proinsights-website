About

ProInsights is the morning briefing that runs in the background while you run the floor. We pull every new review, run them through Claude AI for sentiment, themes, and named dishes or staff, then send you the one email and the one WhatsApp message that tell you what changed since last night.

◆

Our vision

An operator should know what changed in their online reputation overnight, in a paragraph they can read with their coffee. No dashboard. No login. No retraining the team.

◆

The method

Every new review pulled from Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com and friends. Translated. Analysed. Cross-referenced against thirty days of pattern data. Delivered before pre-shift.

◆

The technology

Claude AI for sentiment + theme detection in eighteen languages. EU-hosted database. Twilio for WhatsApp. SerpAPI for review aggregation. Brevo for email delivery. No black box.

What's in every issue

Six things land in your inbox at 8am, every weekday, in a single email. Each was added because a real operator asked for it.

01AI Morning Brief

Three sentences telling you what changed since yesterday. What's improving, what's slipping, the one thing to act on first. Generated fresh by Claude every morning from the actual reviews.

02New reviews, ranked by what matters

Any new 5-star that names a staff member by name. Any new 1-2 star with a pre-drafted diplomatic reply for your team to edit and publish. Sensitive content flagged for manual handling.

How it works

Three steps to your first report.

AYou name your venues and your competitors

You give us your Google Business Profile, your TripAdvisor URL, and (for hotels) your Booking listing. You name five to ten competitors that actually keep you up at night. Fifteen minutes on a single onboarding call.

BWe pull and analyse, every dawn

Every morning before sunrise we fetch all new reviews. We translate where needed. We score sentiment. We extract themes, dish mentions, named staff. We cross-reference with the last thirty days. Claude writes the three-line brief.

CYou read the email and the WhatsApp

8am, your timezone, every day. One email. One WhatsApp message. Read it over coffee. Brief the team in pre-shift. Move on with the day. There is nothing to log into. There is no software to install. There is no dashboard to babysit.

Why this exists

I run F&B for a hospitality group in Cyprus. For two years I tried Revinate, ReviewPro, and a Google Sheet that broke every other Monday. None of them did the one thing I actually needed — tell me what changed overnight, in a paragraph I could read with my coffee, in time to brief the team before service. So I built it. This is the morning email I wished I'd had in 2024.
